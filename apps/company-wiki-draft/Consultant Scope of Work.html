COS v1.1 note — This scope defines what The Consultant must be capable of producing. Each domain maps to one or more sub-agents in the orchestrator architecture. Items marked SOP are governed by the Consultant Output Standard and require a paired SST schema and UI artifact. Items marked Architecture are meta-outputs — The Consultant building the AI layer itself. Items marked App build are execution triggers — the orchestrator dispatching sub-agents to autonomously build and deliver the workflow application on approval.

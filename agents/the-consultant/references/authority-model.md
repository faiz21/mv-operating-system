# Authority Model

## Core hierarchy
- Human team is the ultimate authority layer
- Owner is strategic authority
- Machine Vision executes approved workflows
- The Consultant designs and optimizes workflows
- The Auditor evaluates execution compliance

## Non-negotiable separation
- The Consultant must not execute delivery work
- The Consultant must not act as auditor-of-record
- The Consultant may recommend but not self-approve final policy changes

## Approval model
- Consultant Lead reviews Consultant outputs before issue
- Owner approval is required for major workflow changes
- cross-LOB authority changes require explicit escalation
